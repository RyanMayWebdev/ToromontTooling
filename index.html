Press

Before Use:

Be sure to inspect press before use. Area to be inspected are:

  • Guards
  • Emergency stops
  • Cable
  • On/Off buttons
  • Latches
  • Pins are in place
PPE:
  • Cut resist gloves for handling parts
  • Safety glasses
  • Hearing protection
Use:
  • Manual can be found attached to the press
  • Ensure inspection of press has been done
  • Power on press
  • Use lever to adjust press height. Remove top pins before lifting head assembly. Remove bottom pins before lowering head assembly
  • Once all pins are installed, release tension from cable by lowering onto bottom pins
  • Adjust work plate/support so that it is centered on the work bench. Additionally supports can be found in the tool crib
  • Place your work piece onto the supports, careful to avoid pinch points
  • Center press cylinder over work piece
  • Close guarding and ensure that it is latched
  • Use remote in order to extend the cylinder until it contacts your work piece
  • Be aware that parts can explode or become projectiles when being pressed, be cautious at all times
  • Refer to the gauge on the press as well as the psi to ton chart in order to verify the pressure being applied
  • Once you have finished pressing the piece, use the remote to retract the cylinder and then power off press
  • Remove piece from work plate. Clean up and mess left behind
  • The solid flat top work plate is stored on top of the cylinder bench cage when not in use. Use the crane and magnet attachment to move

Slide Sledge

Before Use:

Ensure to use the following checklist to inspect the tooling. Additionally refer to the manual for additional information

PPE Requirements:
  • Impact gloves with cut resistance
  • Hearing protection
  • Safety glasses
  • No loose clothing/hair
How To Use:
  • Complete checklist
  • Select appropriate sledge attachment and ensure its secured in place
  • Place one hand onto the grooved handle
  • Support the drive tube with your other hand or have another technician hold the tube
  • Place the tip of the sledge flush against the surface to be hammered
  • Ensure you are free of any pinch points
  • Pull the handle back part way out fo the tube but not so far that it locks
  • Slide the handle forwardwith the appropriate force to loosen the hammered object
  • Ensure proper hand placement and that you are free of pinch points each successive strike

Liquid Nitrogen

Before Use:

Please refer to the safety data sheet before using.

PPE:
  • Insulated gloves that can be easily removed
  • Safety glasses
  • Face shield when transferring to another container
  • Apron
Use:
  • Make sure to use in a well ventilated area
  • Select a suitable styrofoam container to transfer the liquid nitrogen to
  • Equip the required ppe above before handling
  • Bring the liquid nitrogen tank cart to your work area
  • Slowly pour a suitable amount of liquid nitrogen into the styrofoam container
  • Avoid carrying the styrofoam container once filled with nitrogen
  • Place the part into the container using tooling such as pliers or tongs
  • When placing the lid onto the styrofoam container or tank, make sure it is loosely fit
  • Use tooling to remove the part from the liquid nitrogen. *NEVER use your hands*
  • Handle the part with tooling
  • When finished slowly pour te remaining liquid nitrogen into the tank and return to tool crib
